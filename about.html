About GlobalMindED

The GlobalMindED Conference sets a new standard for collaboration among schoolsystem worldwide. This collaboration brings more current and dynamic practices to our students in the United States and around the world.

Unique Activities


Idea cafe:

Sit down for coffee with innovators who can tell you how they launched their ideas.

Breakout Sessions:

Hands-on sessions where ideas are put in action.

Network with International Educators:

Connect with other educators and create a support group that spans the world.
